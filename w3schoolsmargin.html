The margin clearns an area baround an element outside the border. The margin doesn't have a background color and is completely transparent. The margin sides top, right, bottom, and left can be changed independently using separate properties. A shorthand margin property can be used to change all margins at once. % and em are valid. Negative values can cause elements to overlap or outside the document.
