Combine border styles

Shorthand combine border styles. If two styles, the first value is top and bottom and the second value is right and left; e.g. border-style: dotted dashed; The shorthand also works with border-width and border-color.

shorthand property no commas border: {border-width, border-style, border-color}

Likewise for each side no commas border-top: {border-width, border-style, border-color}
